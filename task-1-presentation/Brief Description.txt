For the task of my Digital Ambassador role I made a one-page infographic called Digital Literacy using Canva. I wanted to make something that would help my friends understand how to use the internet professionally.

The infographic has three parts to make it easy to understand

 Definition It says what digital literacy is. It is not about knowing how to use technology but also about using it in a smart and safe way.

 Safe Internet Practices This part has a list of things we should do to stay online like using strong passwords and being careful what we post.

 Email Etiquette I put some tips for sending emails like using clear subject lines and being polite.

I learned a lot from making this infographic. The hardest part was deciding what to include and what to leave out. As a Digital Ambassador I had to pick the things that're most important for students. I also had to make sure the infographic looked good and was not too crowded. I found out that digital literacy is not about being safe online but also, about communicating clearly. Digital Literacy is really important. I think I did a good job of showing that in my infographic. Digital Literacy is something we all need to know about.
